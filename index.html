Dissertation

I'm examining the relationship between literature and politics. More specifically, I'm studying how writers from Black America, the Caribbean, China, and India constructed anti-imperial ideas in their poetry and prose by drawing on a late-Victorian artistic movement called Decadence.

In Fall 2019 and Spring 2020, I was the Instructor of Record for a themed undergraduate expository course titled ENWR 1510 Writing and Critical Inquiry: Writing About Digital Forms: Instagram, Wikipedia, and Podcasts.
